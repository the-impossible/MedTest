Why Choose MedTest?

The aim of the research work is to develop a Web-Based Student Medical Test Scheduler and Result Issuing System to eliminate student waiting time during registration.

Get Started

Objective 1

To design a web based medical test scheduler that enables admitted students schedule online for their medical tests conveniently

Objective 2

To implement this system that also serves as a result issuer

Objective 3

To evaluate the benefit and the advantages this system will have over the pre-existing traditional way of scheduling these medical tests
